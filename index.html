Ahmed Haj Ahmed

Intelligence is an emergent property of systems that learn. I'm interested in building those systems.

My work sits at the intersection of research and engineering—developing evaluation frameworks, training novel models, and building infrastructure that pushes the boundaries of what AI systems can understand and generate.

Research Interests

Reasoning & Model Evaluation

Developing frameworks to measure and improve how language models reason, decompose problems, and chain thoughts. Building synthetic data to teach models structured thinking.

Multilingual Evaluation & Cross-Lingual Transfer

Developing evaluation frameworks and transfer learning techniques that enable models to generalize across languages and cultural contexts.

Multimodal AI

Audio-based generative AI, cross-modal understanding, and developing datasets for under-explored modalities in state-of-the-art models.
